This is actually not about my business, but Mrs M's (side) business which is called Clean My Horse Rug.

Clean My Horse Rug is a family owned dedicated horse rug washing service based in Lancashire. We are within easy access of all motorways so we can offer our services to farms and yards all over Lancashire. Our services include reproofing using Nikwax solutions and all items are washed in an antibacterial wash. We also offer a repair service.
